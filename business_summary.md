# Business Summary

## Objective
Analyze customer purchase and engagement behavior to identify trends and provide practical recommendations for business improvement.

## Executive Summary
The analysis shows that revenue performance is strongly influenced by repeat customers, weekend purchasing behavior, and variation across marketing channels and product categories.

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: **$208,814.81**
- Total Orders: **1,186**
- Unique Customers: **313**
- Average Order Value: **$176.07**

## Top Insights
### 1. Repeat customers are the core revenue engine
Repeat customers account for **51.3%** of total revenue. This suggests that customer retention may produce a stronger payoff than focusing only on new customer acquisition.

### 2. Weekend shoppers spend more
Average order value rises from **$171.28** on weekdays to **$188.02** on weekends. The business can use this pattern to time promotions and product pushes more effectively.

### 3. Performance differs by channel and product mix
The strongest channel by revenue is **Paid Search**, and the highest-performing product category is **Electronics**. Concentrating budget and testing efforts here could improve efficiency.

## Recommendation
The company should prioritize:
1. retention campaigns for repeat buyers  
2. weekend-focused promotions  
3. optimization of the best-performing acquisition channels and categories
